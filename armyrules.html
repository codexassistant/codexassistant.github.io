Ways 40K AI can help

Rule Lookup: Provide quick and precise references to specific rules when asked. Users can ask the chatbot about a rule, and it will provide the relevant information, such as rules for specific units, movements, or actions.

Strategy Suggestions: Depending on the state of the game and the army composition, the chatbot could offer strategy suggestions. This would involve complex pattern recognition and some level of machine learning, but it could be a great feature for beginner players.

Dispute Resolution: Sometimes, players have disagreements over rules interpretation. An AI chatbot could provide authoritative guidance and explain why certain interpretations are correct or incorrect based on the rulebook's text.

Rule Explanation: It can provide detailed explanations of rules for beginners. For example, a user can ask how the cover system works, and the chatbot will explain it step-by-step.

Game Scenario Simulation: For advanced application, it can simulate certain game scenarios and provide an interpretation of how the rules apply to that specific scenario.

Updates and Amendments: When changes are made to the rules, or when new editions or expansions come out, the AI could provide summaries of these changes or simply provide a quick reference to new or modified rules.

Army Building: The chatbot could assist in building armies, advising on points costs, and ensuring that the army is legal according to the game's rules.

Lore Knowledge: Besides the game's rules, the chatbot could also be a repository of Warhammer 40K lore. Warhammer has a rich backstory, and this could add another layer of engagement for players.

Tutorial Guidance: For new players, the chatbot could guide them through playing their first few games. This could be a step-by-step guide to the different phases of a turn, or advice on general tactics and strategies.

FAQs: Maintain a regularly updated list of frequently asked questions and provide answers to common queries.
