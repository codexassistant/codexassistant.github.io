Munitorum Field Manual AI

The Munitorum Field Manual Version 1.2 is a comprehensive guide that serves as a valuable resource for Warhammer 40,000 players. This document contains the most up-to-date points values for all the factions in the game, allowing players to accurately determine the points cost of each unit in their army.

Within the manual, each unit entry provides the points cost increments based on the size of the unit. This means that as the unit grows in size, the points cost will increase accordingly. In some cases, there may be a range of points costs for a unit depending on the lower and upper limits of its starting strength. Players are required to pay the maximum points cost if their unit starts the game with more models than the minimum limit.

To further aid players in understanding the points system, the Munitorum Field Manual directs them to pages 55-56 of the Core Rules, where they can learn more about using points to muster their army effectively.

This manual encompasses all units supported for Warhammer 40,000 at the time of its publication, including those available from Forge World. It supersedes any previously published points values and will be periodically updated to ensure game balance and reflect the latest publications. Players can easily identify new and revised entries as they are marked in red.

The Munitorum Field Manual Version 1.2 serves as an essential tool for players, providing them with the necessary information to build balanced and competitive armies within the Warhammer 40,000 universe.
