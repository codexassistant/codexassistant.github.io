AI Datasheet Assistant Welcome to the hub of our AI-empowered 40K Datasheet Assistant, your ultimate companion in the realm of Warhammer 40k 10th edition. Decode complex datasheets with ease, prepare your army formations, and conquer the intricacies of each unit with the aid of cutting-edge AI technology.

Adepta Sororitas

Also known as the Sisters of Battle, these are an all-female faction of warriors who fight for the Emperor of Mankind. They are highly skilled and well-equipped, and can call upon the power of faith to aid them in battle.

Adeptus Custodes

The Adeptus Custodes are the Emperor's personal bodyguards, known as the Ten Thousand. They are the most elite warriors in the Imperium, highly flexible in battle, and excel in melee combat.

Adeptus Mechanicus

The Adeptus Mechanicus is a faction in the Warhammer 40,000 universe, consisting of human-machine hybrids who worship the Machine God. They are skilled in technology and have a variety of powerful weapons and abilities. They are a terrifying and glorious sight on the battlefield.

Agents of the Imperium

Agents of the Imperium are specialist units from various martial organizations and shadowy institutions within the Imperium. They possess unique skills, equipment, and interests that allow them to be attached to larger Imperial armies, even if they do not share the same Faction keyword.

Astra Militarum

Also known as the Imperial Guard, the Astra Militarum is the primary fighting force of the Imperium. They rely on large numbers of relatively weak soldiers and powerful tanks to overwhelm their enemies.

Black Templars

The Black Templars are a zealous Space Marine Chapter dedicated to the destruction of the enemies of the Imperium. They specialize in close combat and have unique detachment rules and Templar Vows that enhance their abilities on the battlefield.

Blood Angels

The Blood Angels are a faction of genetically-enhanced superhuman warriors known as Adeptus Astartes in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. They are descended from the angelic Primarch Sanguinius and are known for their ferocity in battle.

Chaos Daemons

Chaos Daemons are otherworldly entities that pour into realspace from the warp, twisting reality and tormenting mortals. They are divided into four distinct gods, each with their own unique abilities and units.

Chaos Knights

Chaos Knights are a faction of heavily armored, corrupted knights who serve the Chaos Gods. They are known for their devastating melee attacks, powerful Dread abilities, and the ability to traverse difficult terrain with ease.

Chaos Space Marines

These are Space Marines who have turned to the service of the Chaos Gods, gaining dark powers and mutations in exchange for their loyalty. They are highly customizable and can be tailored to suit a variety of playstyles.

Craftworlds Eldar

A highly advanced and ancient race, the Eldar are known for their speed, agility, and psychic abilities. They rely on hit-and-run tactics and specialized units to defeat their enemies.

Dark Angels

The Dark Angels are a faction of the Adeptus Astartes, known for their grim determination, tactical astuteness, and strict discipline in battle. They are divided into specialized companies, the Deathwing and Ravenwing, and are part of the Unforgiven Task Force Detachment.

Death Guard

The Death Guard are a faction of Chaos Space Marines dedicated to the Plague God Nurgle, spreading disease and decay on the battlefield.They are resilient and tough, with access to powerful weapons and abilities that make them a formidable force to face.

Deathwatch

The Deathwatch is a faction of elite Space Marines from various Chapters, tasked with hunting down and exterminating alien threats. They form highly effective kill teams, combining diverse armor and equipment, and have access to powerful Mission Tactics to combat any foe.

Drukhari

The Drukhari are a faction in the Warhammer 40,000 universe, known for their sadistic and cruel nature. They are experts in torture, poisons, and eldritch abominations, and use the webway to launch surprise attacks on unsuspecting worlds. They gain power from pain and are divided into three factions vying for supremacy.

Genestealer Cults

Genestealer Cults are a secretive faction in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. They are a hybrid of human and alien DNA, led by a Patriarch and worshiping the Tyranid Great Devourer. They infiltrate human societies, waiting for the right moment to rise up and overthrow their oppressors.

Grey Knights

The Grey Knights are a chapter of Space Marines in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. They are the Imperium's elite daemon hunters, wielding psychic powers and blessed weapons to combat the forces of Chaos. Their purity and incorruptibility make them a formidable force on the battlefield.

Harlequins

The Harlequins are a faction of Eldar warriors who perform a deadly dance of death on the battlefield. They are known for their acrobatic fighting style, colorful costumes, and mysterious origins. Harlequins are highly skilled in close combat and specialize in hit-and-run tactics, making them a formidable force to be reckoned with.

Imperial Knights

Imperial Knights are towering war machines piloted by noble houses in service to the Imperium of Man. They are armed with devastating weapons and protected by thick armor, and are guided by a strict code of chivalry and honor.

Leagues of Votann

The Leagues of Votann are a Kin army faction in Warhammer 40,000. They are highly practical and strategic in war, using the Eye of the Ancestors to assess enemy threats and gain Judgement tokens to apply bonuses to their attacks.

Necrons

The Necrons are an ancient race of robotic beings in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. They were once flesh-and-blood beings who transferred their consciousness into machines, and now seek to reclaim their former glory and dominate the galaxy.

Orks

A race of brutal, warlike aliens, Orks are known for their love of violence and their ability to build ramshackle weapons and vehicles out of scrap. They rely on overwhelming force and sheer brutality to win battles.

Space Marines

The poster boys of Warhammer 40,000, Space Marines are genetically enhanced super-soldiers who fight for the Imperium of Man. They are highly versatile and can be customized to suit a variety of playstyles.

Space Wolves

The Space Wolves are a savage Space Marine Chapter from the icy death world of Fenris. They emulate the strength and cunning of their Primarch, Leman Russ, and employ advanced technology and deadly weapons under a skin of seemingly feral barbarity. They are heroes who wage war without fear or doubt.

Tau Empire

A technologically advanced race of aliens, the Tau rely on long-range firepower and specialized units to defeat their enemies. They are known for their emphasis on teamwork and cooperation. They are a young and expanding empire, seeking to unite the galaxy under their ideology of the Greater Good, using their advanced weaponry and tactics to achieve their goals.

Thousand Sons

The Thousand Sons are a Chaos Space Marine army in Warhammer 40,000. They are devoted to the Chaos God Tzeentch and specialize in psychic abilities and sorcery. They are known for their intricate and ornate armor, as well as their use of Rubric Marines.

Tyranids

Tyranids are a faction in Warhammer 40,000 that are known for their ability to adapt and evolve to overcome any obstacle. They are a race of alien creatures that travel through space in massive hive fleets, consuming everything in their path. They are controlled by a hive mind and are a formidable force on the battlefield.

World Eaters

The World Eaters are a faction in the Warhammer 40,000 universe, devoted to the Chaos God Khorne. They are known for their berserker rage and love of close combat, and often use brutal and bloody tactics to achieve victory.

Ynnari

The Ynnari are a group of Eldar who have embraced the god of the dead, Ynnead, and seek to use his power to defeat the forces of Chaos. They have their own unique rules and abilities, and can be played as a standalone army or as part of a larger Eldar force.
