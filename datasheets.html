Decode complex datasheets with ease, prepare your army formations, and conquer the intricacies of each unit with the aid of cutting-edge AI technology.

Adeptus Custodes

The Adeptus Custodes are the Emperor's personal bodyguards, known as the Ten Thousand. They are the most elite warriors in the Imperium, highly flexible in battle, and excel in melee combat.

Acastus Knight Asterius

The Acastus Knight Asterius is a towering, heavily-armored Imperial Knights vehicle armed with a karacnos mortar battery, twin conversion beam cannons, and volkite culverins, capable of devastating fortresses and clearing ramparts. It also has the Deadly Demise and Code Chivalric abilities.

Chaos Knights

Chaos Knights are a faction of heavily armored, corrupted knights who serve the Chaos Gods. They are known for their devastating melee attacks, powerful Dread abilities, and the ability to traverse difficult terrain with ease.

Drukhari

The Drukhari are known for their sadistic and cruel nature. They are experts in torture, poisons, and eldritch abominations, and use the webway to launch surprise attacks on unsuspecting worlds. They gain power from pain and are divided into three factions vying for supremacy.

Grey Knights

The Grey Knights are the Imperium's elite daemon hunters, wielding psychic powers and blessed weapons to combat the forces of Chaos. Their purity and incorruptibility make them a formidable force on the battlefield.

Eldar Aeldari

A highly advanced and ancient race, the Craftworlds Eldar or Aeldari known for their speed, agility, and psychic abilities. They rely on hit-and-run tactics and specialized units to defeat their enemies.

Necrons

The Necrons are an ancient race of robotic beings in the Warhammer 40,000 universe. They were once flesh-and-blood beings who transferred their consciousness into machines, and now seek to reclaim their former glory and dominate the galaxy.

Orks

A race of brutal, warlike aliens, Orks are known for their love of violence and their ability to build ramshackle weapons and vehicles out of scrap. They rely on overwhelming force and sheer brutality to win battles.

Titans

Titans are the largest and most powerful units in all of Warhammer 40,000, and each one inspires awe in all who behold them. Though rarely seen on all but the largest battlefields, they make a splash wherever they show up.

Tyranids

Tyranids are known for their ability to adapt and evolve to overcome any obstacle. They are a race of alien creatures that travel through space in massive hive fleets, consuming everything in their path. They are controlled by a hive mind and are a formidable force on the battlefield.
